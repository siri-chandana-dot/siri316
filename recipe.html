Burrata bruschetta

Ingredients

  • fresh burrata ball
  • 1 baguette or rustic Italian bread, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved or quartered
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Instructions

Prepare the Bread Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Place bread slices on a baking sheet. Drizzle lightly with olive oil and toast for 8–10 minutes until golden and crisp. Rub one side of each slice with a garlic clove while still warm. Tomato Topping In a bowl, toss the cherry tomatoes with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Let sit for a few minutes to release juices. Assemble the Bruschetta Tear the burrata into chunks and place on the toasted bread slices. Spoon the marinated tomatoes over the burrata.
